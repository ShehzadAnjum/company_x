## B. Personal profile vs. Company Page

A personal profile named after a company can be flagged under LinkedIn's real-name policy and
can't use follower/product/ad features. The lead-gen plan calls for a **Company Page**
(`/company/veloce-ai`), brand-owned, with real people (Hamza) as the personal profiles that post
and engage. **Recommended setup:**
- **Company Page** `Veloce AI` = the brand home (copy below).
- **Hamza's personal profile** = the human face; lists "Founder, Veloce AI"; does the outreach.
- Retire/relabel `/in/veloce-ai` (or keep it as a lightweight placeholder).
