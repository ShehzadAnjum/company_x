Our process

From first call to a live FTE.

A streamlined, honest path — we scope the work, wire it into the tools you already use, and only ever put what's real into production.

01

Find the bleeding role

We map the job draining your hours — the inbox, the complaints queue, the food cost, the camera no one watches — and the channels it already runs on.

02

Scope the FTE

We define exactly what the Full Time Equivalent owns end to end, the policy it follows, and the points where a human stays in command.

03

Wire it in

We connect it to your existing stack — Gmail, WhatsApp, cameras, ledgers, dashboards — and ground it in your real data and playbooks. No rebuild.

04

Deploy to production

The FTE goes live: watching, reasoning, acting and reporting 24/7 — with approval gates wherever the stakes are high.

05

Measure & scale

Every action is logged and explainable. Once it's proven on one role, we point the same platform at the next.
