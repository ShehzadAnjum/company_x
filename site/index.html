01 The thesis

A headcount you don't have to grow.

An AEVUM FTE isn't a chatbot you bolt onto a SaaS. It's an AI worker built agent-first — it watches the channels your business already uses, reasons over your real data, and does the job. No salaries, no shifts, no attrition. One worker, or a thousand, spun up in minutes.

/ 01

Cost out, not headcount up

Each FTE absorbs the repetitive, always-on work of a human seat — often several — at a fraction of the loaded cost.

/ 02

Machine-grade perfection

Consistent, auditable, tireless. Every action is logged; nothing gets bored, distracted, or skips the checklist at 3am.

/ 03

Native AI, long memory

We've tracked and adopted every wave of AI as it landed. These products are designed around autonomous agents — not retrofitted with one.
