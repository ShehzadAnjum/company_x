## 1. What we sell

**Veloce AI equips businesses with _autonomous FTEs_** — AI agents that own a job end-to-end and
run 24/7, with a human in the loop where it counts. Tagline: **"Autonomous Intelligence. Always On."**

Every product is sold as **a role it replaces**:
> _"The AI [role] — works 24/7, replaces ~N hrs / one seat of [function], with human-in-the-loop control."_
> Execution loop: **Watch → Reason → Act → Report.**

**Our edge = radical honesty.** The market is flooded with "AI agent" vaporware. We win by showing
**what runs today vs. what's on the roadmap** — proof over promise. This is non-negotiable in every
post, email, page, and demo.

---

## 2. Ground rules (read before posting anything)

1. **Brand-led, not personal.** We market as **Veloce AI**, fronted by **Hamza** as the public face.
   Shehzad operates behind the scenes; his personal LinkedIn is **off-limits** for promotion.
2. **Never name any specific client or employer.** Proof comes from our **own demo** (see §5), never
   a named customer.
3. **Sell only what runs.** Lead messaging uses the real, working capability. Roadmap features are
   labelled as roadmap. Honesty is the whole brand.
4. **Owned product only.** Everything we sell is our own clean-room codebase — no client data,
   config, or branding in it, ever.

---

## 3. Who we sell to (beachhead ICP)

**Multi-branch consumer businesses in Pakistan with high complaint / CX volume** — F&B chains,
retail, telco, e-commerce.

- **Buyer persona:** Operations / CX / Brand manager who feels complaint-handling pain daily.
- **Wedge product:** the **AI Complaints Officer** (our most proven capability).
- **Expansion:** once a logo trusts us, widen to the other agents (cost control, security, back-office)
  and to Gulf / global markets.

> One vertical, one proven product, one buyer — then widen. Do **not** pitch all 25 products at once.

---

## 4. Pricing direction

Anchor against a **salary**, not against software:

- **Setup / deployment fee** (one-time) — covers the done-for-you rollout.
- **Monthly per-agent retainer** — the recurring "FTE salary."
- Frame: _"replaces ~N hrs / one seat at a fraction of the cost, runs 24/7."_

Exact numbers TBD together. The hybrid motion = **land the service, grow into recurring product.**

## 5. The lead engine (run in this order)

### Stage A — Plug the leaks (Week 1–2)
The live site can't capture a lead today (dead `mailto:`). Fix first:
- Contact form on veloce-ai.com.
- "Book a 20-min demo" (Calendly) CTA.
- Analytics + LinkedIn/Meta pixel (free) for measurement + retargeting.
- Remove broken "lab" links in production.

### Stage B — Build owned proof (Week 2–3)
- **"DemoEats" demo** — the AI Complaints Officer running on a fictional brand + synthetic data.
  100% ours, shareable forever, no client risk. **This is our #1 proof asset.**
- **Demo video(s)** of the agent actually working.
- **Readiness scorecard** lead magnet — _"Which of your roles can an AI run 24/7?"_ (captures + qualifies).

### Stage C — Turn on demand (Week 3+)
| Priority | Channel | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **#1** | Hamza-fronted **LinkedIn** + Veloce **company page** | Build-in-public + autonomous-FTE POV, 3×/week |
| **#1b** | **Cold outbound** (email + LinkedIn DM) | 50–100 beachhead accounts, references the owned demo |
| **#2** | **Referrals / warm intros** | Warmest leads; free |
| **#3** | **Demo videos** (Loom / YouTube) | Conversion engine; feeds every other channel |
| **#4** | **SEO product pages** | Long game, compounds |
| **#5** | **Light paid retargeting** (~$150–250/mo) | Only chases people who already visited |
| Later | Communities, webinars, agency/reseller partnerships | Scale levers once the core converts |
