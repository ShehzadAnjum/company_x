Full shareable plan: **`MARKETING_PLAN.md`** (repo root; written for Shehzad + Hamza). Live working board:
`tasks/todo.md`. Lessons: `tasks/lessons.md`. This file holds the durable decisions.

**Offer & motion:** autonomous-FTE products sold as *the role they replace* ([[identity-and-positioning]]).
Motion = **hybrid** — land a done-for-you deployment, grow into recurring product. Pricing direction:
one-time setup fee + monthly per-agent retainer ("the FTE's salary"), anchored to a human salary (numbers TBD).

**Beachhead ICP:** multi-branch consumer businesses in **Pakistan** (F&B, retail, telco, e-commerce) with high
complaint/CX volume; buyer = Ops / CX / Brand manager. Wedge product = the **AI Complaints Officer**. Expand to
Gulf/global later. (Geography decided: start local → expand.)

**Hard constraints (load-bearing):**
- **Never name the employer/client** the flagship was deployed for. Sell a clean-room rebuild we own — zero
  client data/config/branding in the sellable product.
- **Brand-led, fronted by Hamza** (independent of the employer). Shehzad operates behind the scenes; his
  personal LinkedIn is off-limits. Reach buyers via off-network channels (cold email, X, communities).
- Proof asset = an **owned "DemoEats" demo** on synthetic data — never the real deployment. *(Stage B, not built yet.)*

**Channel priority (budget <$500/mo, organic-heavy):** (1) Hamza-led LinkedIn + Veloce company page ·
(1b) cold outbound to the beachhead · (2) referrals · (3) demo videos · (4) SEO product pages · (5) light retargeting.

**Stack now live on veloce-ai.com:** lead form (Web3Forms) + Calendly demo booking + GA4; team email
`sales@ / info@ / *@veloce-ai.com` → fan-out Worker → 3 inboxes. See [[deployment]] · [[worklog-and-todo]].
